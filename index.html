Stock Trade Calculator

A calculator for determining the outcome of stock trades.

Points of interest:

  • Utilized AngularJS's data binding and event-handling to provide live calculation as inputs change.
  • Implemented front-end using CoffeeScript and Less.
  • Flat design implemented using modern CSS3.
